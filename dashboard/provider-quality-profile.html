What's Included / What's Not

Methodology transparency

What We Measure

  • Billing patterns and procedure volumes
  • Peer comparison against reference CPT code set
  • Charge ratios and billing flags
  • MIPS/QPP government quality scores
  • Board certification and licensure status
  • Public review sentiment and volume
  • Scheduling access and telehealth availability

What We Can't Measure

  • Diagnosis-level care quality or clinical outcomes
  • Prescription appropriateness and medication decisions
  • Patient-level care journeys and longitudinal outcomes
  • Commercial insurance activity (not in public claims data)
  • In-patient hospital quality metrics
  • Interpersonal communication and bedside manner
  • Referral network quality and coordination
