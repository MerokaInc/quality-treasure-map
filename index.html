Pre-check gate

Step 1. Safety Gate

Should this provider be in the marketplace at all?
Binary check: pass = scored, fail = excluded. No weighting, no partial credit.
NPDB State medical boards PECOS / OIG exclusion list
Pass → proceed to scoring
25%
Step 2

Credentials & Training

Is this provider qualified in their claimed specialty?
NPPES ABMS PECOS
Board cert is primary signal. Highest ERISA weight (duty of prudence).
25%
Step 3

Patient Experience

What do patients actually say about this provider?
Google Healthgrades Doximity RateMDs
Normalised to 0-100. Review volume = confidence signal, not score input. No reviews = null, not penalty.
15%
Step 4

Access & Availability

Can patients actually get seen?
Practice onboarding (self-report)
Hours, wait times, telehealth, languages. Additive bonuses. Schema is the 30-day deliverable.
20%
Step 5

Clinical Quality (MIPS)

Has the government assessed this provider's quality?
CMS QPP Care Compare Part B claims
Three situations: strong score → weight it. No score → null (size signal). Low score → cross-reference first.
Phase 2
15%

Step 6. Clinical Outcomes

Care journey, preventive care, readmissions. Requires live employer claims data. Gold standard, not needed for initial bar.
